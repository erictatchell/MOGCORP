Privacy Policy

What 100GIGZ Collects and Why

100GIGZ is a private archive experience. If you use Google sign-in, the site may receive the basic Google account details you authorize, including your email address, profile name, and profile photo.

The site also stores the content you choose to create or upload, such as archive posts, profile details, comments, and media files. That information is used only to authenticate access, display member identity, manage archive permissions, and operate storage and moderation inside the service.

Content shared inside the archive may be visible to other authorized users with access to the same areas. 100GIGZ does not sell personal information and does not use Google user data for advertising.

Account records and uploaded content are processed through Google Firebase services used to run the site. If you want to request access, correction, or deletion of your information, email erictatch@gmail.com.

Terms of Use

Basic Rules for Access

By using 100GIGZ, you agree to use the site only for lawful access to the archive and only with credentials or invitations intended for you.

You are responsible for the content you upload or post. Only share media, names, and materials that you own or are allowed to distribute, and do not attempt to bypass the vault, abuse member access, or interfere with the service.

Access may be suspended or removed at any time to protect the archive, other members, or the platform. The service is provided on an as-available basis without guarantees of uninterrupted availability.

Contact

Questions, Privacy Requests, and Support

For support, account questions, privacy requests, or Google sign-in compliance questions, contact erictatch@gmail.com.

Deletion and access requests can be sent directly by email with the subject line that best matches your need, including data deletion request or account support.

Google Compliance

How Google Account Data Is Used

Google sign-in is used only to verify identity and support account access inside 100GIGZ. The site uses the basic account identity information needed for authentication and profile display, including standard email and profile details provided through Google and Firebase Authentication.

100GIGZ does not request access to Gmail, Google Drive, Google Photos, or other broad Google data sets. Google account information obtained through sign-in is not sold, not used for advertising, and not shared except as needed to operate the site infrastructure and authorized member experience.

The service uses layered access controls including the vault password, authenticated user access, and Firebase-backed data rules. Questions about Google data use or compliance can be sent to erictatch@gmail.com.
