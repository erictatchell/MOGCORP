What 100GIGZ Collects

100GIGZ is a private archive experience. If you use Google sign-in, the site may receive the basic Google account details you authorize, including your email address, profile name, and profile photo.

The site also stores the content you choose to create or upload, such as archive posts, profile details, comments, and media files.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to authenticate access, display member identity, manage archive permissions, operate storage, and support moderation inside the service.

100GIGZ does not sell personal information and does not use Google user data for advertising.

Google and Firebase Data

Account records and uploaded content are processed through Google Firebase services used to run the site. Google sign-in is used only to verify identity and support account access inside 100GIGZ.

The site uses the basic account identity information needed for authentication and profile display, including standard email and profile details provided through Google and Firebase Authentication.

Visibility and Sharing

Content shared inside the archive may be visible to other authorized users with access to the same areas. The service uses layered access controls including the vault password, authenticated user access, and Firebase-backed data rules.

Access, Correction, and Deletion

If you want to request access, correction, or deletion of your account information or uploaded content, email erictatch@gmail.com.

This privacy page is public and does not require vault access or Google sign-in.
